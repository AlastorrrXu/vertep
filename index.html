Dr. Olha Khometa

Dr. Olha Khometa is an esteemed professor at the University of Illinois specializing in Slavic folklore and Ukrainian cultural studies. As the guiding professor for our Vertep project, she brings a wealth of knowledge on Ukrainian traditions, bridging academic rigor with cultural appreciation. Her expertise not only enhances our understanding of Vertep but also fosters a deep respect for Ukrainian heritage.
