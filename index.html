About Vertep

Vertep is a traditional Ukrainian nativity play that blends folk storytelling with religious themes. This cultural heritage reflects Ukraine's rich historical traditions and is celebrated during the Christmas season.

Organizers

Professor Olha Khometa is an esteemed professor at the University of Illinois specializing in Slavic folklore and Ukrainian cultural studies. As the leader and project organizer of our Vertep project, she brings a wealth of knowledge on Ukrainian traditions, bridging academic rigor with cultural appreciation. Her expertise not only enhances our understanding of Vertep but also fosters a deep respect for Ukrainian heritage.
